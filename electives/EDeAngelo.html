Psychology (Honors & AP)

Ms. Erin DeAngelo

Teacher Response

What students will learn:

A variety of psychological topics including the structure of the brain, psychological disorders and their treatments, theories of emotion and personality, and memory.


In-class Workload:

Classwork consists of a blend of online and paper assignments as well as interactive projects.


Homework:

Homework consists of handwritten flashcards for every unit.


Materials Required:

Index cards are a must!


Tests & Quizzes:

Multiple choice tests are given at the end of a unit.


Self-Studying:

Yes! It is extremely important to be studying and reviewing the vocabulary flashcards every night.


AP Exam:

The AP exam consists of two sections. Section one is 100 multiple choice questions in 70 minutes that is worth 2/3 of the overall score. Section two is 2 free-response questions in 50 minutes that is worth 1/3 of the overall score.


Honors VS AP:

Honors students receive a curve on exams and they have less writing assignments compared to AP students.
