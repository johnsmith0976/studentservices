Ms. Sophia Zulueta

AP Spanish Language and Culture

Teacher Response

What students will learn:

Speaking, listening, reading and writing in Spanish.


In-class Workload:

Daily in class assignments. Several grades a week.


Homework:

Homework is reading, speaking, listening to Spanish.


Materials Required:

Binder with loose leaf paper, headphones, blue or black pens, folder.


Tests & Quizzes:

Spelling quizzes once a week, AP Classroom quizzes weekly, graded essays.


Self-Studying:

You must speak Spanish outside of the classroom.


AP Exam:

A long exam that has multiple choice questions based on reading articles and tables and audio clips. A written short essay and long argumentative essay. A short speaking conversation and a 2 minute speaking presentation.


Additional Comments:

Attendance and participation is crucial.
