Ms. Sophia Zulueta

Spanish 2

Teacher Response

What students will learn:

Review of basic Spanish 1, present tense and common phrases, plus past tense.


In-class Workload:

Most of my work is done in class. I expect weekly participation in Spanish. Studying of notes and vocabulary should be done nightly. Attendance is crucial.


Homework:

Finishing notes that you could not do in class, studying vocabulary. Nightly review of notes.


Materials Required:

Binder with loose leaf paper, pens, pencils, highlighter, folder and index cards optional for vocab review.


Tests & Quizzes:

In lieu of quizzes, I do mini projects that show what you have been learning.


Self-Studying:

Yes, practice with the new material.


Additional Comments:

If you pay attention in class, take notes, review notes daily and participate, you will be successful.
