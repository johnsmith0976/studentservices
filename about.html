About

Our purpose:

McFatter Student Services is designed to give students a heads up about their classes and teachers. We use the opinions of past and present students to describe both the class and teaching styles of teachers at the school. We also give general information about the school to provide viewers with a better understanding of the various aspects of our campus.

Why we created this website:

We created this website because we wanted an "A" for this project.

About our creators:

Our creaters, Nathan, Patrick, and Andy decided to create a website to help the students of Mcfatter choose their programs and to learn more about the teachers and clubs of the school. Nathan came up with the idea after we brainstormed for a new website that could benefit the students of McFatter. Patrick was the lead programer as he had already learned HTML and was on CSS by the time we started making the website. Andy designed many of the page layouts of the website and chose the color scheme.
